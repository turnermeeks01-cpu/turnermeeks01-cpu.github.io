The Red River Pours Model

How We Work

Red River Pours operates on a dry-hire model — meaning we bring everything except the alcohol. You supply the drinks; we handle every detail of the bar experience.

You Bring the Booze. We Handle the Rest.

Liquor laws require a permit to sell or furnish alcohol at private events. Rather than pass that complexity — and cost — on to you, we keep it simple: you purchase the alcohol yourself, and Red River Pours provides the trailer, the staff, the setup, and the service.

This model actually works in your favor. You get full control over what's stocked and how much you spend on the bar tab — without any markup from us on the alcohol itself.

We Take It From There

Once the alcohol arrives at your venue, we take ownership of the bar. Setup, service, and cleanup — all handled so you can focus on enjoying your event.

How the Day Flows

1

We Send You a Shopping List

After booking, we'll build a tailored alcohol shopping list based on your guest count, event duration, and preferred drinks. No guesswork on your end.

2

You Purchase the Alcohol

Pick it up at your preferred local retailer and have it ready at the venue. We'll coordinate the logistics with you in advance.

3

We Arrive, Set Up, and Pour

Red River Pours rolls in early, gets the trailer staged and stocked, and has the bar ready well before your first guests arrive.

4

We Handle Breakdown and Cleanup

When the last pour is made, we break everything down and leave the space exactly as we found it. Any unused alcohol is yours to take home.
